Who am I?

Howdy! My name is Cameron Becker and I am a percussion sophomore in CSU's undergraduate music education program. I am a MSOE teaching artist for percussion and have taught at several local programs in Fort Collins, Loveland, Greeley, and Denver. I have performed with the University Symphony Orchestra, toured internationally with the Wind Symphony, and have served leadership in the CSU Marching Band.

I have performed on snare drum with the Blue Knights Percussion Ensemble, Blue Knights Drum & Bugle Corps, and Phantom Regiment. I also connect with our local music scene by playing drums for local indie and funk bands. I also teach private percussion lessons to students in Loveland and Denver.

Music & Percussion Services

Offering private lessons, live performance bookings, and full orchestral percussion coaching — tailored to fit your needs. Available for all ages and experience levels, both online and in-person.

$25–$50/hr or Event

  • Private drum set lessons for all styles, from beginner basics to advanced techniques.
  • Concert snare drum, mallet percussion (marimba, xylophone, glockenspiel), and timpani instruction.
  • Full orchestral percussion training, including accessory percussion and excerpt preparation.
  • Marching percussion coaching for snare, tenor drums, bass drum, and cymbals.
  • Music theory tutoring, including sight-reading, rhythm studies, and aural skills.
  • Private lessons in classical, jazz, pop, and world percussion traditions.
  • Audition preparation for honor bands, All-State, college entrance, and drum corps.
  • Coaching for percussion ensemble, solo recital pieces, and chamber music.
  • Conducting basics and score reading for young musicians and aspiring music educators.
  • Assistance with percussion setup, maintenance, tuning, and recording techniques.
  • Live performance bookings: solo percussion, drum set gigs, or full band collaborations.
  • Customized lessons tailored to your musical goals, available both online and in-person.
